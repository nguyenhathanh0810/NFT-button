WHAT IS DOOMED SOULS ARMY?

DSA is an NFT collection of 2,501 Doomed Souls that wander the Ethereum Blockchain. Doomed Souls Army have a total of +160 possible traits, including skin, mask, soul type, eye gear, clothes and much more.

WHY GET A DOOMED SOUL?

The Wasteland of Souls is a very lonely place and having some company will avoid you from going mad! Also, by getting a Doomed Soul you have a voice in the community and can help guide the direction of the project.

Owning a Doomed Soul grants you special perks for future projects, plus 30% of the ETH raised on the secondary market will return to the DSA holders through special raffles, contests and more.
